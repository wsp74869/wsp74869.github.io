Top 3 Guitarists of All Time

By: Stewart Preston

The Rolling Stone Magazine assembled a panel of voters including top guitarists and other experts in the field to vote on the top guitarists of all time. According to this panel to top 3 guitarists of all time are Jimi Hendrix(1), Eric Clapton(2), Jimmy Page(3).

Each of these guitarists helped expand the world of music. Jimi Hendrix manipulated the guitar, the whammy bar, and the studio in ways that had never been done before within the rock music industry. Eric Clapton created a blues-based sound that is still to this day the core of many modern rock bands. Jimmy Page was the lead guitarists of one of the best rock guitar bands of all time, Led Zepplin.

Of all the guitarists throughout the history of music these three were rated to be the top three by many other musicians and experts:

  1. Jimi Hendrix
  2. Eric Clapton
  3. Jimmy Page
